Research Projects

  • Pharmacokinetic parameters, (Aragen Life Sciences):

    Multi-class high-performance deep learning approach to predict levels of pharmacokinetics parameters (Vdss and PPB) for FDA-approved oral drugs as a case study. The project utilized molecular descriptors computation and feature engineering techniques, leveraging tools like Pedalpy, RDKit, and Schrödinger's QikProp for descriptor generation.

  • Masters Thesis, (Central University Of Punjab):

    Early diagnosis of dopamine transporter imaging-confirmed Parkinson’s Disease using machine learning and artificial neural networks. Gradient Boosting achieved high accuracy (93%) in differentiating PD patients from healthy controls. Identified genes influencing the models highlight potential roles in PD diagnosis.
