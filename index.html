Research Projects

Here are some of my key research projects:

  • Pharmacokinetic Parameters Prediction (Aragen Life Sciences, 10/2023 - 04/2024): I undertook the project "Multi-class High-performance Deep Learning Approach to predict levels of Pharmacokinetic parameters (Vdss and PPB) - FDA approved oral drugs as a case study. " This study developed predictive models for pharmacokinetic parameters using molecular descriptors computation, feature engineering, and machine learning techniques. Tools used include Pedalpy, RDKit, and QikProp of Schrödinger.
  • Early Diagnosis of Parkinson's Disease (Masters Thesis, Central University of Punjab, 01/2023 - 07/2023): Explored the potential of machine learning algorithms for diagnosing Parkinson's disease using gene expression profiles. Multi-layer perceptron achieved 93% accuracy in differentiating PD patients from healthy controls, highlighting the potential for early detection of PD through gene expression data.
